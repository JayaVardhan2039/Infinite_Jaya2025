MUSIC GENRES

Jazz
The kind of music that makes you lean back, close your eyes, and get lost in the flow. Think coffee shops, smoky lounges, and soulful sax solos.
Rock
Loud, bold, and unapologetic. Whether you're smashing air guitars or brooding under headphones, rock is the sound of rebellion and raw emotion.
Classical
Elegant and timeless. A warm hug for your brain perfect when you need focus or want to feel like royalty sipping tea in a grand hall.
Hip-Hop
Rhymes that speak truth, rhythm that moves you, and stories that hit deep. From poetic flows to punchy beats, this genre wears a crown.
Folk
Rustic and real. Songs that feel like handwritten letters from distant lands simple, soulful, and soaked in heritage.
